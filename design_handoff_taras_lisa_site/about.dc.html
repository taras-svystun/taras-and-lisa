About us

We're Taras and Lisa — partners in life, and partners in whatever's next. We got together over a shared love of moving through the world with as little planning as possible: hitchhiking across countries with no fixed route, saying yes to sports we'd never tried, and collecting friends everywhere we stopped.

That restlessness turned out to be useful. We spend our days now teaching the things we picked up along the way — how to communicate like adults, how to read a mountain, how to fall down a slope with less damage, how to keep moving when things get hard. We're still doing all of it together.
